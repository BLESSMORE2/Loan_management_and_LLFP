Currency Overview

Below is a list of all the currencies that our system supports for currency conversion, powered by the Exchange Rate API. These currencies are listed with their official ISO 4217 three-letter currency code, their name, and the country they belong to. This ensures accurate exchange rates across our financial operations.

Be aware that some older currency codes may be deprecated and replaced over time due to economic changes. To learn more about ISO 4217 currency codes, please visit ISO 4217 on Wikipedia.
